Charity Concert Project

As a musician, my primary civic goal is to participate in a charitable concert to raise funds for local associations, specifically Une couverture pour l'hiver (UCPH). This initiative bridges the gap between artistic expression and social solidarity.

Situation: Requirement of 40 hours of civic service to promote social awareness among ENSEEIHT engineering students.

Task: Coordinate and execute a live musical event dedicated to helping homeless people through UCPH.

Action: Managing artist booking, venue logistics, and communication strategies to maximize donations and awareness.

Result: Ongoing project — Validating part of my civic hours while providing tangible support to those in need.
