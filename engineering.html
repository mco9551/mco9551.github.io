Signal Equalization Project

SituationAcademic project at ENSEEIHT centered on digital transmission chains and signal perturbations.

TaskDesign a simulation to model channel disturbances (noise and ISI) and implement equalization filters to recover the transmitted data.

ActionModeled the transmission chain in MATLAB. Implemented Zero-Forcing (ZFE) and Minimum Mean Square Error (MMSE) equalizers. Analyzed performance through Bit Error Rate (BER) curves, constellations, and eye diagrams.
